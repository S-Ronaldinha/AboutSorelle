Technical Assistant, Black Girls Code New York

Black Girls Code is a non-profit organization that focuses on introducing

technology to African American girls. As a recurrent volunteer, i served as

a classroom technical assitant.I facilitated their coding experience by helping

them troubleshoot any problems they may have stumbled upon either with their

code or the editor we were using.

Food packing, Feeding South Florida

A lot of people in the USA suffer from food insecurities,which means,they

do not know when their next meal is going to be,and/or are not even sure if

they are going to have a meal for the day. As a recurrent volunteer at Feeding

South Florida, I ran through the donated food stock, checking expiration dates,

and repackaging so they could ship it to those in need.

Serving Food, The Relief Bus New York

The Relief Bus is a mobile outreach to homeless people where they provide

homeless people with freshly cooked meals,and information they may need to help

them get off the streets. As a frequent volunteer, i served food,and engaged in

conversations as some people just needed someone to listen.I am glad i was there for them
